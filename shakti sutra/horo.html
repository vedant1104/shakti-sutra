HOROSCOPE CONSULTATION

What is Horoscope?





Horoscope is the practice of determining information about the nature and course of a person's life based on the study about the positions of the planets right from the moment that the person was born. It starts from the analysis of the individuals “birth chart”, which is like a diagram and that depicts where the planets were at the exact moment of his/her birth. Consultations involving a client’s birth chart are useful for determining wide-ranging information about different areas of their life.


What is Horoscope Consultation?

Seeking an experience and proven person who has the skills and abilities to predict your future by going through your birth chart or horoscope which is a unique map of positions of the stars and planets at the exact time and place of your birth, it is the astrologer’s expertise to identify and then provide the insight about it.
